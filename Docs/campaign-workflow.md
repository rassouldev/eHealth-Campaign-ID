# Campaign Workflow

## Traditional Campaign Process

1. Voucher design
2. Printing
3. Distribution
4. Household delivery
5. Data collection
6. Reporting

This approach is often slow and resource intensive.

---

## Digital Campaign Workflow

1. Campaign configuration
2. Digital voucher generation
3. Distribution
4. Field verification
5. Data synchronization
6. Monitoring and reporting

---

## Advantages

- faster deployment
- improved traceability
- reduced duplication
- simplified monitoring
