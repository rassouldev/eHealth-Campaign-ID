# eHealth Campaign ID White Paper

## Abstract

Public health campaigns in low- and middle-income countries often face operational challenges such as manual voucher creation, duplication risks and high logistics costs.

The **eHealth Campaign ID system** introduces a scalable digital infrastructure enabling secure generation of campaign identifiers and digital vouchers.

---

## Background

Large-scale campaigns such as:

- vaccination campaigns
- malaria prevention
- LLIN distribution
- seasonal malaria chemoprevention

often require managing **millions of beneficiaries**.

Paper-based systems frequently result in:

- duplication
- loss of vouchers
- operational inefficiencies
- high logistics costs.

---

## Proposed Approach

The proposed infrastructure introduces:

- digital beneficiary identification
- automated voucher generation
- secure identifier validation
- structured campaign reporting
